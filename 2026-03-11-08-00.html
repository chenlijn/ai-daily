📄 Top 10 arXiv Papers

Time window: March 10, 2026 | 10 selected papers for detailed analysis

1. Agentic Critical Training (ACT)
https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08706
Problem: Traditional imitation learning for LLM agents only teaches "what to do" without understanding "why."
Method: ACT introduces RL paradigm training agents to identify the better action among alternatives.
Results: Average improvements of 5.07 points over imitation learning and 4.62 over RL.
Conclusion: Genuine self-reflection can be trained through RL rather than injected via distillation.
2. RetroAgent: From Solving to Evolving via Retrospective Dual Intrinsic Feedback
https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08561
Problem: Standard RL paradigms favor static problem-solving over continuous adaptation.
Method: Hindsight self-reflection with dual intrinsic feedback tracking subtask completion and language feedback.
Results: Surpasses GRPO by +18.3% on ALFWorld, +15.4% on WebShop, +27.1% on Sokoban.
Conclusion: Retrospective self-reflection enables agent evolution beyond static problem-solving.
